Scrum Master → QA + Securityround 1 review

QA: 1 Blocker + 5 Major found (in-memory repo lost state, cookie SameSite gap, double-API-prefix). Security: 1 High + 5 Medium (no rate limiting, cookie cross-origin gap).

Scrum Master → Senior Backend + Senior Frontendfix round

Blocker fixed (singleton repo), rack-attack rate limiting added, cookie policy made env-aware, double-prefix URL bug fixed, codepoint-accurate char counter, request-cancellation race fixed. CP11 (Twilio, unverified pending client creds) landed alongside.

QAround 2 verification

All 8 round-1 findings independently re-checked against real code: 8/8 verified fixed. One new Major surfaced (Rack::Attack's in-memory cache won't share state across multiple processes — flagged as a pre-deployment follow-up).

Code ReviewerCR loop · iteration 1

System-wide review: 4 Major (unsafe param coercion, zero service/gateway/repo specs), 6 Minor, 4 Nitpick. Routed back to Senior Backend Dev.

Senior Backend Devfix

Type-guarded to_number/body, added service/gateway/repository unit specs, pinned rack-cors, hardened .gitignore.

Code ReviewerCR loop · iteration 2 → PASS

All 4 Majors verified resolved. Fresh holistic pass found only 2 cosmetic Nitpicks. Loop closed — well under the $300 cap.
